How we started

  • 2000 - 2002

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We started with an idea to help, to help all the animals arond the world as much as we can. It was initially just a thought, but with the help of other students and professors, we made this a reality.

  • March 2002

    SJRC is Born

    The San Jacinto Rescue Center (SJRC) was formed in 2002 by a group of students with a shared concern for both animal welfare and the public health. Since opening our doors, we have remained true to our mission of helping all animals in need, regardless of species or breed. At our Shelter, we care for and find permanent, loving homes for dogs, cats and other companion animals.

  • December 2012

    Transition to Online Service
